Benefits of Studying at Shikshadeep

Effective Study

Mastering effective study techniques is key to academic success. Our resources offer tailored strategies to optimize learning, from time management to active engagement, empowering students to excel in their studies.

Extracurricular Activities

Our college encourages students to engage in extracurricular activities as an integral part of their holistic development. Through participation in clubs, sports teams, and volunteer initiatives, students cultivate leadership skills, foster teamwork, and create lasting memories.

Professional Development Workshops

We offer regular workshops and seminars focused on building essential skills for future success in the professional world. From communication skills to leadership development, these workshops provide practical knowledge and experiences that complement students' academic studies and prepare them for their careers.

Student Engagement

Our college emphasizes student engagement through a variety of activities and opportunities. From clubs and organizations to community service projects, students have the chance to explore interests, develop leadership skills, and build friendships outside of the classroom.

Champions Den: Shikshadeep Sports!

Where Champions Are Made

Cricket Triumph: Team Scores 149 Runs to Victory!

Shikshadeep Cricket Team secured a thrilling victory by scoring 149 runs in just 15 overs. The standout performance came from the opener, who smashed an unbeaten 85 runs off 44 balls, steering the team to an impressive win.

Basketball Team Makes Mark as Runner-Up in Intense Finals Showdown!

With victories against powerhouse teams like Arniko and Kantipur, Shikshadeep's basketball team showcased their skills and determination, reaching the finals and clinching the prestigious runner-up title.

From College Talent to National Star: Dipesh Kandel Joins National U19 Cricket Squad!

Dipesh Kandel, hailing from Shikshadeep, has been chosen to represent the country in the National U19 Cricket Team. A proud moment for Shikshadeep as one of its own makes it to the national stage.

Girls' Football Team Crowned Champions!

The girls' football team from Shikshadeep, achieved victory, securing the championship title in an impressive display of skill and teamwork. Their success reflects the dedication and hard work they've put into their sport.
