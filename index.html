Champions Den: Shikshadeep Sports!

Where Champions Are Made

Cricket Triumph: Team Scores 149 Runs to Victory!

Shikshadeep Cricket Team secured a thrilling victory by scoring 149 runs in just 15 overs. The standout performance came from the opener, who smashed an unbeaten 85 runs off 44 balls, steering the team to an impressive win.

Basketball Team Makes Mark as Runner-Up in Intense Finals Showdown!

With victories against powerhouse teams like Arniko and Kantipur, Shikshadeep's basketball team showcased their skills and determination, reaching the finals and clinching the prestigious runner-up title.

From College Talent to National Star: Dipesh Kandel Joins National U19 Cricket Squad!

Dipesh Kandel, hailing from Shikshadeep, has been chosen to represent the country in the National U19 Cricket Team. A proud moment for Shikshadeep as one of its own makes it to the national stage.

Girls' Football Team Crowned Champions!

The girls' football team from Shikshadeep, achieved victory, securing the championship title in an impressive display of skill and teamwork. Their success reflects the dedication and hard work they've put into their sport.
