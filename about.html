About Us

Shikshadeep

Welcome to Shikshadeep, located in the heart of Biratnagar, Nepal. Since 2053 B.S., we have been proud to serve our community by offering a comprehensive and enriching educational experience.

Nestled in the vibrant city of Biratnagar, our college provides students with access to a diverse array of cultural, recreational, and professional opportunities. With state-of-the-art facilities and a picturesque college setting, Shikshadeep offers an inspiring environment for learning and growth.

At Shikshadeep, we are committed to academic excellence and student success. Our dedicated faculty members are experts in their fields and are passionate about mentoring and guiding students on their educational journey. With small class sizes and personalized attention, we ensure that every student receives the support they need to thrive.

In addition to our strong academic programs, Shikshadeep offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports teams, and community service opportunities. These activities not only enhance the student experience but also foster leadership skills, teamwork, and personal development.

As an educational institution, Shikshadeep is dedicated to fostering a culture of diversity, equity, and inclusion. We believe that a diverse learning community enriches the educational experience for all students and prepares them to succeed in an interconnected world.
